# ArenaX Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** October 1, 2023

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to ArenaX ("Game", "Service", "We", "Us", or "Our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mech battle game ArenaX. 

By using ArenaX, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access or use the Game.

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Username, email address (optional)
- **Payment Information:** Transaction history, payment method (processed by third-party payment processors)
- **Contact Information:** If you contact support, we collect your email and message content

### b. Gameplay Information
- Player statistics (level, wins, losses)
- In-game purchases and transactions
- Equipment and inventory
- Match history and battle outcomes

### c. Technical Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address and approximate location
- Game performance data
- Crash reports and error logs

### d. Children's Information
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Game
- Process transactions and send purchase confirmations
- Improve gameplay experience and develop new features
- Prevent fraud, cheating, and security issues
- Respond to player inquiries and support requests
- Send promotional communications (with opt-out option)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- **Service Providers:** Payment processors (Stripe), cloud hosting (Render), analytics providers
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with a merger or acquisition
- **With Your Consent:** When you direct us to share information

## 5. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers outside your country. South African users: We comply with POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act). EU users: We comply with GDPR requirements.

## 6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Access controls
- Fraud detection systems

Despite these measures, no security system is impenetrable.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:
- Account information: Until account deletion
- Payment records: 7 years for legal compliance
- Gameplay data: Until account deletion
- Technical logs: 30 days

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have:
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to correct inaccurate information
- Right to delete your data
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@arena-x.com.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies for:
- Session management
- Game performance monitoring
- Fraud prevention

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some game features may not function properly.

## 10. Third-Party Services

The Game contains links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Key third parties:
- **Stripe:** Payment processing (view their privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy)
- **Valr:** Crypto transactions (valr.com/privacy-policy)
- **Render:** Cloud hosting (render.com/privacy)

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes through in-game notifications or email.
